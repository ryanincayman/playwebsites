This is how it all started.

When Ryan and Cornelia met, sparks flew instantly and they both knew that they were attracted to each other.

It definitely wasn’t that easy.

Before they met, Cornelia had been on 46 first dates while Ryan had not dated at all for six years. Did Ryan still remember how to date? And what was different about this 47th date for Cornelia?

We know Ryan and Cornelia eventually became the Unicorns, but their first encounter is not what you would expect. Their story began with a match on Tinder…
